Compound ingredients

If a food contains that contribute 5% or more to the final food, then all the ingredients and additives in that compound ingredient must be declared. If a food contains a compound ingredient that contributes less than 5% to the final food, then only allergens and food additives require listing.

For example: The ingredient list would show the dark chocolate chips in a muffin as: dark chocolate chips [sugar, cocoa mass, cocoa butter, natural vanilla flavour].
