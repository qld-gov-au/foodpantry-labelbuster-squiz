For example:

  • An ingredient of a flavouring substance; or
  • A volatile ingredient which is completely removed during processing; or
  • added water that:
    • is added to reconstitute dehydrated or concentrated ingredients; or
    • forms part of broth, brine or syrup that is declared in the statement of ingredients or is part of the name of the food; or
    • constitutes less than 5% of the food; or
  • a substance or food that is used as a processing aid.

Note: An exempt ingredient does not include:

  • Added caffeine.
