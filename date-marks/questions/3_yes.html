Use-by date

These foods must be labelled with a because they may become unsafe to eat after the use-by date has passed. A food may look, smell and taste ok but be unsafe to eat.

Best-before date

A is required for all foods with a shelf life of 2 years or less that do not need a The best-before date is the date up to which the food remains with good appearance, smell and taste, and keeps any qualities for which a claim has been made.
